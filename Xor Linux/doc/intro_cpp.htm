Your challenge: The maze and its inhabitants

    You really want to defeat Xor and enter the mazes of Xor ? Congratulations !
    What will be expected to you ? Each maze is a 32 x 32 field, but you can only see an 8 x 8 part of it !
    15 levels will wait for you, level 1 is very simple - to make you hungry for more !
    Each level you will master will increase your fame and your competence in solving future challenges
    - and there are lots of it !  But now, take a look at the inhabitants of Xor:



    This is you - in the land Xor !



    This is your twin - by pressing the 'C' key you can switch between you and your twin!




    This is a wall. It's a natural barriere that you cannot walk through ! But you can destroy it - how ? You will see...   




    This is a mask. Collect all of them !




    And then, escape through this exit !




    This is a map. Torn into four pieces, you must collect the four parts to have a complete map.
    Collect one piece, and you can see a fourth part of the maze - by pressing the 'M' key.
    Collect all four and you can see all !




    This is a wave. You can only walk through it from top to bottom or from bottom to top !




    These are dots. You can only walk through it from left to right or from right to left !

    These are all inhabitants of level 1. But in the next levels, you will get to know these:





    This is a chicken. You can push it up or down, if there's enough space !
    And it always wants to chase from right to the left, if there's no wall or other item that
    could prevent it from chasing !




    This is a fish. You can push it to the left or the right, and it falls down, if there's no wall or other items that prevents it !




    This is a doll. You can push it, and it will move towards the direction you want !




    This is a bomb. If a chicken or a fish (or a falling bomb or acid) hits the bomb, it explodes and the items
    at the left and the right will be destroyed ! Very useful for removing a wall and other barriers !
    Like a fish, you can move it to the left or to the right and it falls down (if it can!).




    This is acid. If a chicken or a fish (or a falling bomb or acid) hits the acid bottle, it crashes and the items
    at the top and the bottom will be destroyed - because acid is corrosive ! Very useful for removing walls
    and other barriers ! Like a chicken, you can move it up or down and it chases to the left (if it can!).




    This is a sad mask. It makes all walls invisible, if you collect it ! Collect another sad mask,
    and the walls becomes visible again !
 



    This is a teleporter. It will transport you to a different part of the maze.



How to play


    After you have installed the program, start it and you will see this :


           


    To start a new game, simply click at 'Game', and then 'Run/Restart'. Or 'Exit', if you
    want to do something different.

          
 


    Click at 'Level' and choose one of 15 exciting levels. Then you can play the selected level !





   
    A very useful function brings you the 'Replay ' menu:
    You can store  all moves you have made in a level by saving it in a file !
    Click at 'Save Replay', and  a file dialog window appears. Type in a file name
    (it's recommendet to name the file with the '.moves ' ending), and it is saved !
    Click at 'Load Replay' to load a '.moves ' file.
     



    Then,  you can click at the diamond-shaped icon and you are in the 'Replay' mode:

   



Now, three icons will appear:
  • Clicking the left one will replay a single move
  • Clicking the middle icon will end the replay mode. Then, you can continue playing
  • Clicking the right icon will replay all moves except the last one: It's an Undo function !  

 The keys
  • Use the 'Arrow' keys to move the player through the maze
  • Use the 'M' key to take a look at the map (if you have collected one or more pieces)
  • Use the 'C' key to change the player 
  • Use the 'Space' key to center the playing field
